Text Cleaner

Clean text online by removing extra spaces, blank lines, duplicate lines, and messy formatting.

About this Text Cleaner

Use the text cleaner to normalize messy pasted content before publishing, importing, or analyzing text data.

How to use

  1. Paste the raw text into the input area.
  2. Choose the cleanup options you need.
  3. Run the cleaner and copy the normalized output.

Example

Helpful for copied logs, spreadsheets, email lists, notes, transcripts, and support text.

FAQ

What can a text cleaner remove?

It can remove extra spaces, blank lines, duplicate lines, and other simple formatting issues depending on the options.

Why clean text first?

Cleaner text is easier to read, compare, import, and reuse in other tools.
