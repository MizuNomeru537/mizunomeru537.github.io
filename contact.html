Contact & Support Guide

This page helps visitors organize a clear support request, bug report, or tool suggestion before sending it through their preferred contact channel.

Tip: include the tool URL, your input example, the result you saw, and what you expected instead. Clear bug reports are far easier to reproduce and fix.

What to include

Bug reports

Share the exact page, your input, the result you got, and whether the issue happens again after refresh.

Tool requests

Explain the task you are trying to complete and why the current tools do not cover it well.

Business inquiries

Say who you are, what kind of partnership or placement you want, and the page or audience you are interested in.

Support expectations

ToolLab focuses on lightweight browser utilities. Requests that are specific, reproducible, and clearly tied to a page on the site are the easiest to review and prioritize.

Before sending a message

  • Check whether the issue is limited to one browser or device.
  • Try refreshing once if formatting or copy behavior looks stale.
  • Include the page path, not just the tool name.
  • Do not include secrets, credentials, or other sensitive information in your message.
